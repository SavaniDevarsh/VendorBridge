# VendorBridge

VendorBridge is a vendor and procurement management platform designed to streamline the complete purchasing lifecycle within an organization. The system enables procurement teams, managers, and vendors to collaborate efficiently while maintaining transparency, accountability, and process control.

## Key Features

### User Authentication & Role Management

* Secure role-based login system.
* Supports multiple user roles including:

  * Administrator
  * Procurement Officer
  * Manager/Approver
  * Vendor
* Access to features and data is controlled based on user responsibilities.

### Vendor Management

* Centralized vendor onboarding and record management.
* Stores important vendor information such as:

  * Company Name
  * Address
  * Business Type (LLP, Pvt. Ltd., Government Organization, etc.)
  * GST Details
  * PAN Information
* Managed by authorized procurement and administration teams.

### Purchase Request & RFQ Management

* Supports procurement requests generated from internal business requirements.
* Procurement officers can create and manage Request for Quotations (RFQs).
* RFQs include:

  * Product or service requirements
  * Quantity details
  * Submission deadlines
  * Selected vendors

### Vendor Quotation Management

* Vendors can submit quotations directly through the platform.
* Quotations include:

  * Pricing information
  * Delivery commitments
  * Additional comments or terms
* Automated notifications keep vendors informed about RFQ activities.

### Quotation Comparison & Evaluation

* Side-by-side quotation comparison interface.
* Helps procurement teams evaluate:

  * Cost competitiveness
  * Delivery timelines
  * Vendor performance indicators
* Simplifies vendor selection and decision-making.

### Approval Workflow

* Multi-level approval process for procurement requests.
* Managers and authorized approvers can:

  * Review requests
  * Add remarks
  * Approve or reject submissions
* Complete approval history is maintained for audit purposes.

### Purchase Order Management

* Approved quotations can be converted into Purchase Orders (POs).
* Automatic PO number generation.
* Centralized tracking of order status throughout the procurement cycle.

### Goods Receipt & Quality Inspection

* Supports procurement fulfillment activities including:

  * Raw material ordering
  * Inward inspection
  * Goods Receipt Note (GRN) generation
  * Pre-Delivery Inspection (PDI)
* Ensures quality and compliance before final acceptance.

### Invoice Processing

* Generate invoices directly from approved Purchase Orders.
* Automatic calculation of taxes and totals.
* Invoices can be:

  * Downloaded as PDF
  * Printed
  * Shared electronically

### Audit Logs & Notifications

* Records all major user actions and workflow events.
* Provides real-time notifications for:

  * RFQ updates
  * Approval status changes
  * Procurement milestones

### Reports & Analytics

* Dashboard-driven reporting system.
* Monitor:

  * Procurement spending trends
  * Vendor performance metrics
  * Procurement activity statistics
  * Operational efficiency indicators

## Objective

VendorBridge aims to digitize and simplify procurement operations by providing a structured workflow from vendor onboarding to final invoice processing. The platform improves visibility, reduces manual effort, and supports data-driven procurement decisions.
